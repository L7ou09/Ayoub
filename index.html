Cats :

• Cats (Felis catus) are small, carnivorous mammals that have been domesticated for thousands of years. They are known for their agility, sharp retractable claws, keen senses (especially night vision), and flexible bodies. Cats are highly independent yet social animals, often forming strong bonds with their owners. They communicate through vocalizations (like meowing and purring), body language, and scent marking. Domestic cats are popular pets worldwide, valued for their companionship, hunting skills (particularly for controlling rodents), and playful behavior. There are also many breeds, varying in size, coat type, and personality.
